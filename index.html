PLAYER FACTS

Lionel Messi

Born a football prodigy, Lionel Messi spent his life growing up in Rosario, Argentina right by the Paraná river. At the age of 16 he joined F.C. Barcelona in which he played for 20 years. Throughout his career, Messi has won 42 trophies, making him (in my opinion) the best soccer player.

Neymar Jr

Neymar was born in Mogi das Cruzes, São Paulo, Brazil. He joined Barcelona in 2013 and then later Paris in 2017. Neymar is mostly known for his great dribbling skills. He and Lionel Messi are known to be great friends during their time in Barcelona.

Christiano Ronaldo

Raised in the Capital of Portugal's island, Maderina, Cristiano spent his young life in poor wealth. transferring through multiple professional teams, cristiano made a total of 824 goals in his entire football career. In fact he only missed 29 penalties.
